Spotify Analysis Dashboard
1. Business Problem

Music streaming platforms like Spotify generate massive amounts of user data, but struggle to:

Understand customer spending behavior
Identify high-value customers
Track artist performance and revenue contribution
Analyze churn rate and customer retention
Decide where to invest in marketing and promotional events

Without proper analysis, it becomes difficult to maximize revenue and improve customer engagement.

2. Goal of the Dashboard

The main objective of this dashboard is to:

Track total customers and overall revenue trends
Identify top spending customers and high-performing artists
Analyze billing patterns across countries
Monitor customer churn rate
Provide insights for marketing strategies like event planning
3. Key Visuals Used in Dashboard
KPI Cards

Used for:

Total Customers
Average Earnings of Top 5 Artists
Total Bill & Invoice
Customer Churn Rate

Why used:
KPI cards provide a quick summary of important business metrics for decision-makers.

Donut Charts

Used for:

Top 5 Spending Customers
Top 10 Billing Countries
Total Bill Distribution

Why used:
Donut charts effectively show percentage contribution and comparison among categories.

Bar Charts

Used for:

Average Billing Country-wise
Top Artist Earnings per Customer
City for Promotional Events

Why used:
Bar charts are ideal for comparing values across categories like countries, artists, and cities.

Horizontal Bar Chart

Used for:

City to throw promotional events

Why used:
Helps in ranking cities based on customer presence or potential engagement.

4. Business Impact

This dashboard helps the business to:

Identify high-revenue customers and target them effectively
Improve artist promotion strategies
Reduce customer churn
Optimize country-wise pricing and billing strategies
Plan promotional events in high-potential cities
Increase overall profitability and customer engagement
5. Key Insights Derived from Dashboard
Insight 1

Total customer base is 20K, showing a strong user base for analysis.

Insight 2

Top 5 customers contribute a significant portion of total revenue, indicating revenue concentration.

Insight 3

Customer churn rate is around 22.73%, which is relatively high and needs attention.

Insight 4

Certain countries contribute more to billing, indicating geographical revenue imbalance.

Insight 5

Top artists generate higher average revenue per customer, showing their strong influence on user spending.

Insight 6

Cities like Berlin, London, and Paris show high potential for promotional events based on customer activity.
